Symptoms Illustration showing acute sinusitis Acute sinusitis Acute sinusitis symptoms often include: Drainage of a thick, yellow or greenish discharge from the nose or down the back of the throat (postnasal drainage) Nasal obstruction or congestion, causing difficulty breathing through your nose Pain, tenderness, swelling and pressure around your eyes, cheeks, nose or forehead that worsens when bending over Other signs and symptoms can include: Ear pressure Headache Aching in your upper jaw and teeth Reduced sense of smell and taste Cough, which might be worse at night Bad breath (halitosis) Fatigue Fever
